FAQ

Questions Local
Business Owners Ask

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long will it take to build my website?+
Most websites are completed within 2–3 business days once I have all content from you. Larger or complex projects may take up to 5–7 days. I'll give you a clear timeline upfront before we start.
Do I need to provide the content and images?+
You can provide your own, or I can assist with basic copywriting and source free stock images. Real photos of your business always convert better and are highly recommended.
Will my website work perfectly on mobile phones?+
Absolutely! Every website I build is designed mobile-first. Since 75–80% of local business customers browse on mobile, this is always my top priority.
Will I be able to edit my website myself?+
Yes! I build on WordPress with an easy visual editor so you can update content without any technical knowledge. Or I can handle all updates as part of a maintenance plan.
What does SEO Optimization include?+
SEO includes keyword research, proper page titles and meta descriptions, fast loading speed, heading structure, image optimization, and Google Business Profile setup — so local customers find you first.
Can you redesign my existing website?+
Definitely! If your current website looks outdated or isn't converting, I can redesign it with a modern look while keeping your existing content and SEO rankings intact.
Do you offer payment plans?+
Yes! I offer a 50% upfront, 50% on delivery structure. For larger projects, a 3-part payment plan is available. Contact me to discuss what works best for you.
